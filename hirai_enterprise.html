About Us

Hirai Enterprise has been a pioneer in the lubrication and hydraulic solutions industry for nearly two decades. Established in Pune, Maharashtra, we are committed to providing innovative and reliable solutions that meet the specific needs of various manufacturing sectors. Our extensive experience enables us to understand the complexities of industrial operations, making us a trusted partner for optimizing performance and ensuring the longevity of machinery.

Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to uphold our reputation for quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. By continually investing in research and development, we stay ahead of industry trends, offering cutting-edge products and services that empower our clients.

Our Expertise

At Hirai Enterprise, we specialize in a variety of lubrication systems and hydraulic piping solutions, designed to address the unique challenges faced by our clients. Our areas of expertise include:

  • Customized Lubrication Solutions: We design and manufacture tailored lubrication systems that enhance operational efficiency.
  • Hydraulic Piping Solutions: Our expert team provides comprehensive hydraulic piping services, ensuring seamless integration with existing systems.
  • Maintenance Services: We offer ongoing maintenance and support to keep your machinery running smoothly and effectively.
  • Consultation Services: Our knowledgeable consultants provide insights and recommendations for optimizing lubrication and hydraulic systems.

Financial Performance

Our focus on quality and reliability has enabled us to establish long-term partnerships with various industries, which has contributed significantly to our sustained growth and success.
